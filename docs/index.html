Features

Features of Valinfo

There are many features of Valinfo that you can explore.

Party Indicator

Now you can know who is play with his friend in same match.

Headshot Percentage

Why he is give me one tap?! Oh, he has a 34% headshot percentage, now you can see headshot percentage of enemy.

Winrate Percentage

See who's grinding, Wondering why that low ranked player is in your lobby, surely they're not getting boosted? Well, it's because they have a high winrate percentage.

Level

you can see level of any profile in match.

Agents

screen for agents of enemy team and party team.

Peak Rank

That man who is got 22/9 K/D can't be Bronze 2! Ohh, he used to be Diamond 3, See who is smurf in game.

Ranks

See ranks of enemy and your teammates in same match .

Rank Rating

Your Rank Rating is the number of points you gain after each competitive game.

Skin Tracking

See who's got your favourite skin, Works for both sides.

RPS

Updates your Discord Game Activity to reflect your match, Updates live.
